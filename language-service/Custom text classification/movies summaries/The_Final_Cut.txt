A maniac bomber is ruthlessly targeting Seattle, claiming civilians and bomb disposal teams alike as apartment blocks and and office complexes collapse under the impact of his ingenious, complex devices. Calling in ex-Bomb Squad man John Pierce  to help them, and using computer assisted disposal techniques and virtual reality simulations, the Squad come to a horrifying realization - the bombs are constructed with tricks and traps intended to kill the disposal teams...and he only person who would know such schemes would be a fellow Bomb Squad officer.
